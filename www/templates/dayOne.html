Refugi de Montmalús 15:00 - 19:00

The Montmalús Hut is next to the Estanys de Montmalús lakes in the parish of Encamp. At an altitude of 2,445 metres, it has 10 places. You can get to the Montmalús Hut by car to Soldeu. Then continue on foot along the ski track to the Coma Estremera Lake (2,392 metres alt.). Then climb la Coma to the pass, just in front and to the left of the Pic de Soldeu passing through els Fontanals, Port Dret and following the Sant Josep river to the hut. The route takes approximately 2 hours and has a climb of 810 metres, with medium difficulty.
